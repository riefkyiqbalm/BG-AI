Our Comprehensive Services

CAD Modeling

From concept to detailed design, we create precise 2D drawings and complex 3D models tailored to your specifications. Our expertise covers mechanical components, product designs, architectural elements, and more. We utilize industry-leading software to ensure accuracy and manufacturability.

  • 3D Solid & Surface Modeling
  • 2D Drafting & Detailing
  • Assembly Design
  • Parametric Modeling
  • Legacy Data Conversion

CAM Programming

Optimize your manufacturing processes with our expert CAM programming services. We generate efficient and reliable toolpaths for CNC machining, ensuring high-quality output and reduced production times. From 2-axis to multi-axis machining, we cover a wide range of applications.

  • CNC Milling & Turning
  • Multi-axis Machining
  • Toolpath Optimization
  • Post-processor Development
  • Rapid Prototyping Support

CAE Simulation

Validate and optimize your designs before physical prototyping with our advanced CAE simulation services. We perform structural, thermal, fluid dynamics, and motion analyses to predict performance and identify potential issues, saving you time and resources.

  • Finite Element Analysis (FEA)
  • Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD)
  • Stress & Strain Analysis
  • Thermal Analysis
  • Kinematics & Dynamics Simulation
